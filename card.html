Meerkat

Meerkats have developed an immunity to the venom of certian snakes and scorpions.

  • Scientific Name: Suricata suricatta
  • Average Length: 35 - 50 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 6 - 7 years
  • Habitat: Southern Africa

Meerkats are highly sociable animals and live in large groups of up to 30 individuals. They are great at digging and build large networks of tunnels and burrows to live in. A meerkat's favorite meal is any type of juicy insect, but they are also known to enjoy the odd lizard or snake.
